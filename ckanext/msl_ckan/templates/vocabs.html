    Keyword selection

      Keywords

      The EPOS multi-scale laboratories community publishes and maintains several vocabularies to harmonize metadata descriptions and in turn improve findability of the data. Currently the following vocabularies are available:

      • Analogue modelling of geological processes
      • Geochemistry
      • Geological age
      • Geological setting
      • Materials
      • Microscopy and tomography
      • Paleomagnetism
      • Pore fluids
      • Rock and melt physics

      Access vocabularies on GitHub

This is done by checking the originally assigned keywords, title and abstract for matches with terms and synonyms within the vocabularies. In enriching keyword sets like this, MSL strives to make datasets more findable. Although we strive for optimal results, the methods remains interpretative and as such can lead to errors.
